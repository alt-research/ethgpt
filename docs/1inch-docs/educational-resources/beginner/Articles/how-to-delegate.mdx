**How to delegate to a resolver:**

First, you will need to stake some 1INCH tokens.

Once you hold st1INCH, click on the "**Earn**" tab at the top of the page, and then "**Delegate**".

![](./assets/Aspose.Words.fae090be-d485-4986-972e-0af80b6cd2b0.001.jpeg)

On the next screen you will see all of the available resolvers that can be delegated to. After choosing the desired resolver, click "**Delegate**".

![](./assets/Aspose.Words.fae090be-d485-4986-972e-0af80b6cd2b0.002.jpeg) Next, click "**Confirm**" delegation, and then sign the transaction in your connected wallet.

![](./assets/Aspose.Words.fae090be-d485-4986-972e-0af80b6cd2b0.003.jpeg) Once the transaction confirms on chain, you will have successfully delegated your Unicorn Power to the resolver.
