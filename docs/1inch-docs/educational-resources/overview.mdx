# Overview

Welcome to our comprehensive collection of articles designed to help you navigate the world of DeFi and 1inch. We have organized our resources into three levels - beginner, intermediate, and advanced - to cater to users with varying levels of expertise.

## [Beginner Articles](./beginner/Beginner)

If you're new to 1inch or DeFi, start here. Our beginner articles provide a solid foundation in understanding key concepts, terms, and basic actions within the DeFi ecosystem. You'll learn about staking, delegating, and protecting yourself from market dynamics like price impact and slippage.

## [Intermediate Articles](./intermediate/Intermediate)

For those who have some experience in DeFi and are looking to deepen their understanding, our intermediate articles offer guidance on using the 1inch dApp and related protocols. You'll find resources on troubleshooting the 1inch API, as well as learning how to use block explorers.

## [Advanced Articles](./advanced/Advanced)

Dive into more complex topics and explore advanced strategies with our collection of advanced articles. These resources are tailored for users who have a strong grasp of DeFi concepts and are ready to take their knowledge to the next level. Learn about reading transaction input data, understanding MEV, and much more.
